Who are we?

We welcome people who have passion in gaming, professional or not, to our club!

Whether you are here for the tournaments, spectating, casual games or just to hang out, we invite all of you gamers out there to join us at DIE HARD GAMING

We host tournaments at our gaming arena for different major esports titles include but not limited to:

  • League of Legends™
  • Dota 2™
  • Counter Strike™
  • Fortnite™
